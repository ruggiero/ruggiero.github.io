Other projects

viXra One

In 2020 I created viXra One, an index of high-quality submissions to the viXra repository. viXra is most often associated with questionable content, but many good papers written by legitimate authors exist in there. The index increases the visibility of those papers by excluding submissions that are not LaTeX documents with references.

TuringBot

TuringBot is a desktop software for symbolic regression. It runs on both Windows and Linux.
